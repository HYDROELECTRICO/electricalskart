Buying Guide

How to Choose the Right Water Purifier?

Answer these 4 simple questions to find the best purifier for your home.

1

Where does your water come from?

Borewell or tanker water has high TDS — you need RO + UV + UF technology. For municipal (tap) water, RO+UV is also the safe choice.

Borewell → RO+UV+UF
2

How many family members?

A 9–12 litre tank is enough for up to 4-5 members. For bigger families or higher usage, choose a 15–19 litre model.

Big family → 15+ litres
3

Want extra health benefits?

Purifiers with Copper and Alkaline technology add healthy minerals to your water — good for immunity and digestion.

Copper + Alkaline available
